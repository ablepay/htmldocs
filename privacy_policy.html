Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 25, 2019

This Member Privacy Policy describes how AblePay Health, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“AblePay”, “our”, “us”, or “we”) collects, uses and shares information about our enrolled members (“you” or “members”). This policy applies to information we collect when you access our enrollment and member portals, located at https://enroll.ablepayhealth.com/apply and https://member.ablepayhealth.com/session, respectively (the “Services”), or otherwise interact with us as described below. We may change this Member Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of this policy, and in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to the homepages of our websites or sending you an email notification). We encourage you to review this Member Privacy Policy whenever you interact with us to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.

This Member Privacy Policy does not apply to any information or data collected by AblePay on our marketing websites or through other channels for marketing purposes. Please find the AblePay Privacy Policy that covers this information or data at https://www.ablepayhealth.com/privacy-policy.

Acceptance Of These Terms

By using the Services, you signify your acceptance of this policy and any changes to this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use the Services. If you are outside of the United States, do not use the Services. Your continued use of the Services following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.

Collection of Information

Information You Provide to Us. We collect information you provide directly to us when you do things such as call us on the phone, enroll for the Services, sign up to receive our emails, or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include: (1) your name, date of birth, email address, physical address, gender, social security number, marital status, employment, income, and desired payment term; (2) if you choose to enroll additional members in the Services, then the names, dates of birth and gender of such individuals; and (3) any other information you choose to provide (collectively, “Personal Information”). We also collect your payment information and certain health information in order to provide the Services, (“Sensitive Information”).

Payment Information. We use secure, third-party payment processors, to process your payment information. When adding a payment method, either Bank or Card, your information is sent directly from your device to a third-party payment processor (please see Terms of Service). The third-party payment processors associate the payment method with your AblePay account, allowing us to complete charges or refunds, without AblePay ever viewing your Sensitive Information.

Health Information. We receive medical bills from you, your health provider, and third-party processors. Any and all health information that is provided to us and/or shared directly from members is protected and covered under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). The sharing of your Sensitive Information is only done with entities that are required to complete authorized transactions on behalf of our members.  We share only the minimum information that is necessary in order to fulfill our duties on your behalf.

Cookies. Our website only requires first-party cookies to be enabled in order to process certain information to provide the Services to you. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of the Services.

Use of Information

We use your information solely to the extent necessary to provide the Services to you, in ways such as: (i) to pay a Member’s claim to a healthcare provider as requested by such Member and to pay AblePay in accordance with a Member’s selected payment method; (ii) for compliance and reporting with HIPAA and other regulations required by regulatory bodies; (iii) to respond to your comments, questions and requests and provide customer service; (iv) to manage your online account(s) and send you technical notices, updates, security alerts and support and administrative messages; and (v) to use as part of the collection process for payments overdue to AblePay.

Sharing of Information

We use your information solely to provide the Services. We may share information about you as follows: (i) with select third-parties as described in this policy who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf; (ii) in response to a request for information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with any applicable law, regulation or legal process or as otherwise required by any applicable law, rule or regulation, including for compliance and reporting with HIPAA; (iii) to a Member’s employer, if such employer is paying for Health Reimbursement Administration (iv) to protect the rights, property and safety of us or any third-party; (iv) in connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing or transfer of all or a portion of our business to another company; or (v) with your consent or at your direction.

We do not sell your information to advertisers or other third-parties.

Control and Access of Your Information

You may update or correct your online account information by logging in to the Member Portal. For further requests please email support@ablepayhealth.com.

Security

We have implemented reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. Despite these measures, however, no security measures are completely impenetrable.
