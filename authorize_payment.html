FAILED TRANSACTION FEE

If AblePay Heath, LLC (“AblePay” or “We”) attempts to collect a payment that you have authorized (whether as a single payment, reoccurring payment or otherwise) and AblePay is unable to collect that payment for any reason, including, without limitation, insufficient funds, you will be charged according to the following fee schedule: $35 for each item (maximum 1 Failed Transaction Fee per day; 3 per 30-day period). We will contact you in the event We are unable to collect a payment. It is your sole responsibility to ensure that all payment information is correct and there are sufficient funds when the next attempt to collect payment is made. Our next attempt to collect payment will be the next business day following the failed attempt. If you have any questions, or require assistance please contact AblePay at 484-292-4000 between the hours of 9:00 am EDT and 5:00 pm EDT or support@ablepayhealth.com. If we are unable to collect the payment on the same item multiple times, you may be charged up to 3 times for that item within a 30-day period.
